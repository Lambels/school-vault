## Properties of metals:

### Physical:
- strong.
- malleable.
- ductile.
- sonorous.
- shiny.
- good conductors of both heat and electricity.
- high mp and bp.
- high density.

### Chemical:
- react with dilute acids to form salts and hydrogen gas.
- react with oxygen to form basic oxides.
- form positiv ions.

### General:
*some are*
- magnetic.
- soft.
- un-reactive.
